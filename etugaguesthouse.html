Our Story

A Place to Rest,
Explore & Feel at Home

Etuga Guesthouse is a family-run haven tucked into Nogoon Urlan Khotkhon, Sukhbaatar district — less than a kilometre from Ulaanbaatar's iconic Sukhbaatar Square.

We've designed every corner for comfort: private bathrooms, balconies with garden or city views, a shared kitchen, and amenities that make longer stays easy. Whether you're here for adventure or business, you'll find Etuga a reliable, welcoming base.
