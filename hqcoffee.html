Airag Espresso
HQ Coffee Signature Drink
Our most talked-about creation — cold espresso meets airag, Mongolia's beloved fermented mare's milk. Bold, creamy, and unlike anything you've tasted before.
🇲🇳 Mongolian Fusion · Exclusive to HQ
Signature Creation

Only at
HQ Coffee

We believe coffee can be a bridge between cultures. The Airag Espresso is proof — a creative collision of specialty coffee craft and traditional Mongolian flavours.

Why it works
Cold espresso's bitterness cuts through airag's natural tartness and richness, creating a perfectly balanced fusion drink you won't find anywhere else in UB.
1
Double shot cold espresso — brewed at full strength and chilled.
2
Traditional Airag — fermented mare's milk, smooth and slightly tart.
3
Layered & served cold — sip together for the full Mongolian fusion experience.
