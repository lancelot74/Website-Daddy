Our Story

A Sanctuary for
Humans & Cats

Cotton Cat Cafe was born from a simple belief: that the gentle presence of cats can transform an ordinary day into something extraordinary. We created Ulaanbaatar's premier cat café to offer both people and cats a warm, loving space to thrive.

Our café is home to rare-breed and rescued cats, all living happy, healthy lives while bringing joy and calm to every visitor who walks through our door.

🐱

Animal Welfare First

Every decision we make centers on the health, happiness, and comfort of our resident cats.

🧘

Mental Wellness

Cat therapy is real — we provide a genuinely peaceful escape from the busy city outside.

🤝

Responsible Interaction

We guide every guest on mindful, respectful ways to connect with our cats.

🏡 Our Home, Your Sanctuary

Nestled in the heart of Ulaanbaatar, Cotton Cat Cafe opened in 2022 with a mission to create Mongolia's finest cat café experience — a place where stressed city-dwellers could find genuine calm in the company of extraordinary feline friends.

From rare breeds to rescued souls, each of our cats has a unique story and a permanent home with us.

🏠 House Rules & Guidelines

1

Booking Recommended

Walk-ins welcome but reservations are encouraged, especially on weekends. Call or message us on Instagram to book.

2

Gentle Interactions Only

No sudden movements or loud noises. Never pick up or chase a cat — let them come to you at their own pace.

3

Hand Hygiene

Please sanitize your hands upon entry and after eating. Hand sanitizer is available throughout the café.

4

Children Must Be Supervised

Children under 12 must remain with an adult. We ask for extra gentleness to ensure the cats feel safe.

5

No Outside Food or Drinks

To protect the cats' diet and health, please only consume items purchased at the café.

6

Photography Welcome

Share your moments! Flash photography is disabled to keep our cats comfortable. Tag us @cottoncatcafe.
